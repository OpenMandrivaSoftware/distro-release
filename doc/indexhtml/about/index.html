Welcome to OpenMandriva
What is OpenMandriva

OpenMandriva Association was established on December 12, 2012 under 1901 French law. It manages some Free Software projects including OpenMandriva Lx, a GNU/Linux operating system that is a free alternative to the wide world known Microsoft Windows and Apple MacOS.

What is OpenMandriva Lx

OpenMandriva Lx is a unique and independent distribution and direct descendant of Mandriva Linux.
